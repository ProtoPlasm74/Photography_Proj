Project description

This is heavily inspired by videographer MKBHD. His motto is "Quality Tech Reviews." For this project, I will be focusing on the placement, background, and lighting for each product. Most of them will be stand alone, but possibly displayed on a model for items such as headphones. The vision will be to have a page for each product.

Plan to have this presented and what I will need

The current page that is displayed and simply a placeholder rendered from a past project, so the final project will look much different. I am hoping to put this on a local server; this would require the host to be running full time, so the page will likely not be up all the time. I have no plans to have a domain for this, but it is a possibility. I would like to use a DSLR for some of the shots for bokeh affects and a tripod for steady shots. Finding a nice workspace will be tricky as well, but I may incorperate nature and indoors for a backdrop. More suggestions are also welcome.
